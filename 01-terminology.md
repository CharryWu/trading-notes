# 01 Terminology
- tick: smallest move on futures chart
- pip: smallest move on forex chart
- point: group of ticks or pips
- lot: size of a trade
- handle: round number of price 整数价位
- Trend: Market moves in one obvious direction, the ending bars are higher/lower than starting bars
- Trading range: Market moves in a range, with no obvious direction
- MTR: major trend reversal
- TTR: tight trading range

All candlestick bars can be identified as trend bars or trading range bars
  - Trend bars: candlestick bars that have long body, body > 50% bar height; can clearly identify directions
  - Trading range bars: candlestick barrs that have small body, body < 50% bar height; shows no obvious directions
  - Perfect doji bars are very rare, and it **carries no more meanings** than regular trading range bars
